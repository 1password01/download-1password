What is 1Password?

1Password is a highly rated password manager that stores your login credentials securely and lets you autofill them when needed. It offers powerful encryption, cross-device syncing, browser extensions, and even a digital vault for sensitive files. Whether you use Windows, macOS, Android, or iOS, 1Password works everywhere.

Why Choose 1Password?

  • Watchtower: Alerts you if your passwords are weak or reused.
  • Browser Integration: Autofills passwords and login forms in Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.
